1. Basic Aggregation (Total Trade Value)
Total Trade Value = SUM('Fact Trades'[trade_value_usd])

2. Basic Aggregation (Total Trades)
Total Trades = COUNTROWS('Fact Trades')


3. KPI / Ratio (Avg Trade Value per User)
Total Unique Users = DISTINCTCOUNT('User Info'[user_id])       (Helper Measure)
Avg Trade Value per User = DIVIDE([Total Trade Value], [Total Unique Users])


4. KPI / Ratio (Avg Daily Price Change %)
Avg Daily Price Change % = AVERAGEX( 'Fact Crypto Prices', 'Fact Crypto Prices'[price_change_pct] )


5. Time Intelligence (Year-over-Year Trade Growth)
YoY Trade Value % = DIVIDE([Total Trade Value] - CALCULATE([Total Trade Value], DATEADD('Date'[full_date], -1, YEAR)),
                           CALCULATE([Total Trade Value], DATEADD('Date'[full_date], -1, YEAR)))


6. Conditional Metric (Top Trading Region)
Top Trading Region = MAXX(VALUES(Region[country]), [Total Trade Value])
